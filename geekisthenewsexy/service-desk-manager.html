THE ROLE

We are looking for a technically astute / experienced IT Service Desk Manager to lead our high calibre team of 15 Support Engineers. This is an exciting new senior management role for Lucidica so there will be plenty of opportunity to make your own mark and work autonomously to make changes to suit Lucidica’s requirements using your creative problem-solving skills. We are looking for a strong leader who can encourage and mentor other members of the team whilst inspiring them to develop and have fun. The main responsibilities will include:

LEADERSHIP, SUPPORT & COMMUNICATION

  • Line management for the Support Engineers based in both London and Kiev and the Service Desk Coordinator
  • Carrying out 1:1’s & setting development plans for the Engineering team and completing quarterly appraisals. You will engage with engineers of varying levels to help them drive forward and achieve their own personal development goals
  • Identifying training needs for Engineers and ensuring the team is continually learning and improving.
  • Working together with the operations manager and Director to deliver the companies vision and growth goals.

    • SERVICE DELIVERY

  • You will be responsible for service delivery excellence. Working with both the engineers and if necessary the clients to ensure a smooth running of the service desk account clients and ensuring our customer satisfaction results remain consistently high.
  • Development of policies, standards and procedures for the IT support team
  • You will be responsible for delivering the Service Desk KPI’s such as Customer Service Satisfaction, Engineer capabilities, divisional success and client retention. You will take ownership of the Service Desk problems and proactively deploy the right technical solution in line with the business requirements.
