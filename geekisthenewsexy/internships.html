We are THE rapidly growing IT support company based in the ‘happening’, vibrant heart of Shoreditch. Being only stone’s throw from Tech City and Silicon roundabout, what bright, aspiring and ambitious character wouldn’t want to work here? Everybody at Lucidica is strongly encouraged to reach their full potential and become the best they can be. You will receive the most generous benefits package of any small business north of the river (well we all think so anyway) and we hold loads of team events for a chance to let our hair down together. Everybody that works at Lucidica has a voice, we are all actively involved in the day to day running of the business and we contribute towards its growth.

The Role:

We’re currently looking for a smart, motivated digital marketing student to help build on our digital marketing strategy here, alongside another small business in the health and beauty industry. The two industries do not traditionally go hand in hand, but this would be a great opportunity for a flexible, self-motivated candidate looking to develop their skills. The successful candidate will want to take on a challenge and have a passion for all areas of Digital Marketing. Here’s some of the responsibilities for the role:

  • Working alongside our Marketing Associate to deliver on all aspects of our digital marketing strategy
  • Building and engaging with social media communities
  • Creating bespoke content for us and our clients (blogs and social media)
  • Engaging in off-page SEO including link-building
  • Working with clients to produce effective email marketing campaigns

You:

Lucidica is always looking for new talent to join us, so we asked everyone here to describe their ideal new team member, this is some of what we came up with:

  • ‘A mixture of everyone already here – A positive, hardworking, talkative, caring, organized, fun, smart, quirky, pleasant and high performing person!’
  • ‘Switched on, funny, Friendly and Passionate about their work.’
  • ‘Passion to learn and a wish to be a valuable member of Lucidica.’

For this Sales Administration role, you will also need to have at least 2 years’ experience in a customer service or Telesales position, a keen interest in people, technology and small business. In addition, you must also possess the following:

  • Exceptional and confident communication skills, flawless standard of professional written English
  • Experience in hitting targets and meeting deadlines
  • A positive, can-do and positive client service attitude
  • Passion for meeting new people and desire to engage with business professionals
  • A willingness to learn and develop

Salary and Work Hours:

Although we can be flexible with the hours you will work, initially we will require you to devote at least 3-6 months’ part time (up to 16- 20 hours per week) paid at the national minimum wage. however there here may be the scope for additional hours during the summer.
