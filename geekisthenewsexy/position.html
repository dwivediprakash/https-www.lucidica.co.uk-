REMUNERATION AND WORK HOURS

The Engineer role is full time (40 hours per week, 5 x 8 hour days)

The salary for the position is £27,000-£32,000 per annum (depending on experience, performance & overtime)

The Lucidica Salary is structured differently to other companies, in a good way! We’re fair in the way we pay, so the more you put in and the better the rewards. The salary is fully flexible and made up of the following things:

  • Base salary
    • As an engineer, your salary will be calculated hourly. Nobody in the 11 years of Lucidica history has ever only taken home their base salary, but instead has the following components to boost their earnings…
  • Performance Bonus
    • You will receive a Personal Development plan once every 3 months and your bonus will be depend on how well you do. The targets are clear and realistic and if you are getting everything done you can add up to 20% on to your base salary.
  • Overtime
    • As a Lucidica engineer, as long as you are meeting your targets your overtime is all paid. Some overtime is even paid at 1.5 x and some at 2 x your hourly rate.
    • Time of in lieu (TOIL). If you do decide to put in some additional hours but would rather have the time back instead of paid out, we can add this to your TOIL bank
  • Commission
    • If you assist in the sales process of taking on new clients you will receive commission. There are no hard sales targets for engineers, but as an expert in your field you will no doubt be able to give sound advice and find the right solutions for a clients’ technology needs.
  • Vitality points & flexible benefits
    • This is our own internal currency & industry leading incentive system. You will receive between £500 & £2000 of points in your first year which can be spent on anything from Tech products, travelcards, paid time off, holidays & spa days
