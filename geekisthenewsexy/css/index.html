Why join us?

Personal Development

Each role has a clear path for promotion. We each have tailored personal development plans, quarterly reviews and generous bonuses are awarded for results. Lucidica pays for exams and pays you for the time off to study.

Fun stuff

A team event is held at least once a month for us all to let our hair down together. We understand the importance of work / life balance and enjoying what we do whilst were here and there are loads of things going on in the office for a bit of welcome downtime.

Benefits System

We are rewarded for being great at what we do and helping the business to grow. You will earn points as you work and can spend them on extra time off, lunches, shopping vouchers, holidays or anything else from the ever evolving catalogue of rewards.
