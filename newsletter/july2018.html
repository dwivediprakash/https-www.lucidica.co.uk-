Product of the Month:

Google Smart Display

Google has partnered with a number of companies (Lenovo, LG, JBL) to bring Google Assistant to a touchscreen, including Lenovo, LG, and JBL. The devices feature a screen rather than just a speaker and will compete with Amazon's Echo Show and Echo Spot devices. Each device will feature something that Amazon's voice assistants do not: YouTube. You can also use things like Google Maps, watch recipes, or go shopping with the visual aid of a screen. Tech Tip of the Month:

Microsoft Word Tricks

We'll be sharing 3 quick MS Word tricks that will help you get things done faster and will increase your productivity.

1- Change the Sentence Case: Select some text in Word and press Shift + F3 quickly change the case of the selection. It will toggle between UPPER CASE, Camel Case and lowercase.

2- Write Anywhere on a Page: Double click wherever you want to type your text and start writing.

3- Convert to Plain Text: When you copy snippet from a web page into Word, all the styles and formatting are retained. There's however an easy way to remove the styling from any block of text in Word - just select the block and press Ctrl+Space Bar and the rich text will be transformed into plain text.
