Tech Tip of the Month:

How to Prevent Your Computer from Overheating

As we are experiencing a heat wave in terms of English weather, it seemed topical to discuss a tip related to preventing your computer overheating. A computer overheating can slow down, freeze (ironically) or permanently break a computer so it's important to make sure this doesn't happen. The maximum temperature a computer should be depends on type, make and model of the computer but most people should try and keep their CPU below 40c. To make show your computers cool as a cucumber, ensure it's not next to an equally hot computer and you clean out all the dust and dirt as often as possible.
