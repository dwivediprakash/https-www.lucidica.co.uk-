Cyber & Email Security Updates from Lucidica

Hey there,
 
In response to increasing threats to small businesses we’re rolling out several new products and services to keep your business safe
 
Over the past year, we’ve seen a massive increase in the cyber threats seen by small businesses. These attacks are getting more advanced and with that, we must advance our security management and back-ups. Below is a list of recommendations that we believe are crucial in this cyber climate:

Automatic Updates


Automatic updates can be slightly frustrating as they can require restarting of your computer. However, they are more important than ever and can prevent an attack slipping through your system.

By default, we’re going to be forcing updates and even removing susceptible programs automatically from your machines. We can tailor these to timeframes if needed, so please contact support or your account engineer if you can have concerns about how this will affect your business.

Email Backup

While Microsoft/Google take the responsibility of email storage and recovery, they largely leave user issues (including hacking & malware) to the end user. There are several gaps in Microsoft’s and Googles policies that can leave your business exposed to risk and can be incrediblytime consuming to resolve. There is a decent article here on why you need backup to Office 365, but please give me a call if you’re in doubt and can walk you through the risks At Lucidica we use Office 365 for our email and due to the increase of attacks we’re carrying out 3rd party backups and we strongly recommend that everyone using Outlook or Gmail does the same. These services do come at an additional cost, but as mentioned we strongly recommend it and the cost of a cyber-attack can be much higher.

You’ll be getting a call from us by the end of this month to talk through options

Enhanced Email Security

The standard level of security within Office 365 & GSuite isunfortunately becoming inadequate for the level needed for businesses. Like back-up, we’ve carefully chosen a 3rd party to supply us with enhanced, intelligent email security to prevent attacks. This enhanced email security has AI learning, anti-phishing, and superior quality scanning. We recommending all clients implement enhanced security on some level and your account engineer will discuss the options with you within the call detailed above.
